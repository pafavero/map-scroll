Map and scroll

This example shows that the scroll (through the mouse wheel) stops when the cursor crosses the map. In fact, the map events of mouse wheel are associated with zoom in and zoom out

Try to scroll this page down to see the problem!

Yes, I know: the interuption of the scrolling is not so drammatic, many web sites have this problem. However, a simple solution is very easy to implement. Why to not use it?

The two examples solution1.html and solution2.html show possible solutions
